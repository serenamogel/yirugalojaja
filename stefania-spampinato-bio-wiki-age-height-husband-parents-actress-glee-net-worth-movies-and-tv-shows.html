    Stefania Spampinato Actress

    After completing her study of arts classes, In 2006 Stefania relocated to London, where her work as a dancer enabled her to travel the world. In 2011, she moved to Los Angeles, where she continued to study acting.

    After taking part in TV series such as Glee and Satisfaction, she joined the cast of Grey’s Anatomy in 2017 as Dr. Carina DeLuca, sister of Dr. Andrew DeLuca, and from 2020 continues to play the same role in the TV series Station 19, the second spin-off from Grey’s Anatomy.

    In 2019, she was picked as the co-star of the comedy The Most Beautiful Day in the World, interpreted and directed by Alessandro Siani.

    Stefania Spampinato Station 19

    Stefania portrays Dr. Carina DeLuca an American action-drama television series created by Stacy McKee for ABC that premiered on March 22, 2018. It is the second spin-off of Grey’s Anatomy (after Private Practice).

    Set in Seattle, the series focuses on the lives of the men and women at Seattle Fire Station 19. It stars Jaina Lee Ortiz, Jason George, Grey Damon, Barrett Doss, Alberto Frezza, Jay Hayden, Okieriete Onaodowan, Danielle Savre, Boris Kodjoe, and Miguel Sandoval.
