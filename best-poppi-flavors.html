    Best Poppi Flavors: Every Poppi Flavor, Ranked

    The best prebiotic soda flavors taste like delicious fruit and soda, not watery vinegar. By this standard, Poppi flavors rule. This is the brand you should be buying if youre looking for a prebiotic soda you actually want to drink.

    30 Sep 2024, 00:00

    The best prebiotic soda flavors taste like delicious fruit and soda, not watery vinegar. By this standard, Poppi flavors rule. This is the brand you should be buying if you’re looking for a prebiotic soda you actually want to drink.

    We tried all the Poppi flavors that are currently available to find out which are the most delicious. Lucky for us, we ended up liking all of them! I’m a little skeptical of the actual health benefits of prebiotic sodas, but I can’t deny that Poppi tastes good either way. If you’re on the prebiotic soda train, check out this list of the best Poppi flavors.

    Poppi!

    Doc PopTarget

    This is the only flavor of Poppi I didn’t enjoy. It just tastes like your run-of-the-mill diet soda and it certainly does not taste like Dr Pepper. Dr Pepper is my favorite soda so I was really disappointed by this. Skip Doc Poppi and buy literally any other flavor of Poppi.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    2.5/10

    Poppi!

    Cherry LimeadeTarget

    Cherry Limeade is a little too cherry forward for me. I wish there was more limeade involved. Cherry fans, however, rejoice! This has a bold flavor you’ll enjoy if you’re already super into cherry.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    7.5/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    Classic ColaInstacart

    I love Coke. I hate Coke knock-offs (and I’ve had a lot of them). This is one of the best “cola” flavored things I’ve ever tried. It genuinely tastes kind of like Coke! It’s not as good, but for a probiotic soda, it’ll do.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    WatermelonTarget

    Watermelon is, well, watermelon! This has a nice, slightly candy-ish watermelon flavor that you’ll enjoy if you like watermelon-flavored things. That being said, I don’t love watermelon-flavored things and I thought this was still one of the more delicious, if not one of the best, Poppi flavors.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    Root BeerInstacart

    This is the best non-soda soda I’ve ever had. It really shocked me. It tastes like root beer! Put this in a frosty mug and you’ll be extremely happy enjoying this with a burger. Good job with this flavor, Poppi!

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    OrangeTarget

    Orange Poppi has an excellent, genuine orange flavor. It’s very nice and simple. It’s like if orange soda was subtle. This is definitely one of the best prebiotic soda flavors out there.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    8.5/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    Strawberry LemonTarget

    Strawberry Lemon is really nice. It’s sweet and citrusy and a little tangy. It tastes like a delicious drink and not a drink with purported health benefits. Don’t get confused, though. This isn’t strawberry lemonade. It’s strawberry lemon. The strawberry is more prominent and you just get a nice hint of lemon.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    9/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    Ginger LimeTarget

    Ginger Lime is like a moscow mule seltzer. Speaking of moscow mules, this would make a great moscow mule if you added a little vodka. It certainly doesn’t need it, though. It tastes delicious on its own. It’s bright and gingery and mildly sweet. I will definitely be buying this Poppi flavor again.

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    9.5/10

    Sporks

    Poppi!

    Raspberry RoseTarget

    This is the best Poppi flavor. It is so light and sweet and refreshing. Rose-flavored drinks are often so floral that they taste like air freshener. This, on the other hand, is perfectly balanced. You could casually drink this in the car on the way to Target or you could have a glass of this in place of wine during a nice dinner at home. It doesn’t really matter where you drink it. Just drink it!

    Credit: Merc / Poppi

    Rating:

    10/10
