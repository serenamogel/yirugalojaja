    Ric Flair Jumping Into The Energy Drink Business

    Ric Flair has decades in the pro wrestling business, and he dipped his toe into many different businesses. Now, fans will have the chance to style and profile just like the Nature Boy, and with the same energy.

    10 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Ric Flair has decades in the pro wrestling business, and he dipped his toe into many different businesses. Now, fans will have the chance to style and profile just like the Nature Boy, and with the same energy.

    Ric Flair has always been renowned for his boundless energy. Even at 74-years-old, he continues to defy expectations, having competed in a wrestling match in Nashville last summer. In addition to his wrestling endeavors, Flair has recently ventured into the cannabis industry, partnering with Mike Tyson and his business team to launch a line of legal cannabis products.

    To keep up Ric Flair’s busy schedule of promotional appearances across the country, Flair is now expanding into the beverage market. He is introducing Wooooo Energy, a mushroom-based energy drink that bears his signature catchphrase.

    Wooooo! Energy by Ric Flair is here. Celebrated by Joe Rogan on The Joe Rogan Experience as ‘F—ing tasty,’ the new mushroom-infused energy drink contains a blend of Lion’s Mane, Cordyceps, Chaga, Turkey Tail, Shiitake, Maitake, and Reishi mushrooms, to support immune and cognitive function, and provide clean energy without jitters or sugar crashes.

    Wooooo! Energy is blended with Taurine, L-Theanine, and Gotu Kola Extract and balances 150 mg of caffeine with a modest 6g of sugar. Available now in Dragon Fruit, Lemon, and Strawberry Banana flavors.

    Following his appearances on the Full Send Podcast and Men’s Health Gym and Fridge series, the public praised the 74-year-old, 16-time Champion, and 2-time Hall of Famer for his energy. Now, with the help of leading chemists and mixologists, everyone can experience Flair’s Wooooo! Energy.

    “‘Wooooo!’ is more than Ric Flair’s iconic catchphrase—it’s synonymous with celebration. It symbolizes energy, excitement, and achievement. It transcended wrestling and is now a part of pop culture,” said Chad Bronstein, President and Chairman of Carma HoldCo. “And with ‘Wooooo! Energy’ we get to share Ric’s limitless energy with the world.”

    Wooooo! Energy is available now in partnership with LGNDS at woooooenergy.com. A six-pack of the icon’s elixir can be yours for $30 for those aged 18 and up.

    We will have to see if this new venture for Ric Flair is as popular and profitable as his marijuana business. He also has his hand in many other investments that are likely paying off in a big way as well.

    For more on this story, and so many more, stay tuned with us at Ringside News. After all, you never know what Ric Flair will do next.
