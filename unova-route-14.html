    Route 14 (Japanese: １４番道路 Route 14) is a route in eastern Unova, connecting Undella Town to Black CityBB2/White ForestWW2. It also provides access to the Abundant Shrine. In Pokémon Black and White, Giallo of the Seven Sages can be found and arrested here.

    Occasionally, the mist on this route and Route 15 will disappear.

    Route description

    A route consisting of a few bridges and a stream that has waterfalls. By correctly navigating the waterfalls and the water, the player will reach the Abundant Shrine.

    In Pokémon Black 2 and White 2, the gate connecting the route to Black CityB2 or White ForestW2 is blocked by a line of men, until the player enters the Hall of Fame.
