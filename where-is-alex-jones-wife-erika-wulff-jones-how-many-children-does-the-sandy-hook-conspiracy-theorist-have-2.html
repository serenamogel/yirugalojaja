    Where Is Alex Jones’s Wife, Erika Wulff Jones?

    Erika Wulff Jones, Alex Jones’ wife, was arrested on Christmas Eve on a domestic violence charge that the right-wing provocateur claims stem from a “medication imbalance.”

    His wife was arrested and booked into an Austin jail around 8:45 p.m. Friday. According to jail records, she is facing misdemeanor charges of assault and causing bodily harm to a family member.

    Erika had not received a bond as of Saturday afternoon. Alex declined to say whether he’d been injured or elaborate on what happened Saturday, other than to say he believes it was related to his wife’s recent medication change.

    According to the TV personality, it is a private family matter that occurred on Christmas Eve. Alex loves and cares for his wife, and there appears to be a medication imbalance.

    The Travis County Sheriff’s Office did not immediately respond to a request for the report on Wulff Jones’ arrest on Christmas Day.

    Erika’s attorney did not immediately respond to a request for comment. Alex stated that his wife’s arrest had nothing to do with his politics and was not motivated by personal hatred.

    How Many Children Does Alex Jones Have?

    Alex Jones and his ex-wife Kelly Jones have three children. They divorced and because of her ex-husband’s behavior, Kelly sought sole or joint custody of their children in 2017.

    Kelly was granted the right to choose where their children would live while he was still allowed to visit them.

    A state district judge denied Kelly’s emergency motion to secure custody of their daughters for the next two weeks in April 2020, following Alex’s rally at the Capitol, where he was mobbed by unmasked supporters and called COVID-19 a hoax.

    Rex Jones, his son, has worked for InfoWars and received media attention for a video criticizing gun control and BuzzFeed News.

    Jones and his current wife, Erika Wulff Jones, have another child. So Alex has four children.

    What Happened To His Ex-Wife Kelly Rebecca Nichols?

    Kelly Rebecca Nichols is a vegan activist born on July 2, 1968. She is currently 54 years old and was born under the sign of Cancer.

    She works for PETA as the head of media operations and public relations, according to Just Richest. She met and married Jones in 2007, but they divorced in March 2015.

    She promoted Jones’ website, InfoWars, by leveraging her experience in media and public relations. Kelly appears to be in good health, and no incidents involving her have been reported.

    Nichols was raised in Travis County by her parents, Edmund Lowe Nichols and Sandra Kay Heiligman, alongside her brother James Edmund and sister Jill Elizabeth. It is believed that both of their parents were involved in the real estate business.
