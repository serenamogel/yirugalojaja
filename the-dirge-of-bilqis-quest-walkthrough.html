    You’ll need to take down a group of Eremites, including Azariq. With Jeht’s help, this shouldn’t be a problem. These are all fairly regular Eremite enemies. Another scene will start after the predictable betrayal, and the group will be standing outside the Ruin Golem. Walk forward to get sucked into another fight – this time, with the Fatui.

    Once all the fighting is over and done with, go back inside the Ruin Golem. Follow the quest markers deeper underground, making sure to unlock the Teleport Waypoint along the way.

    You’ll eventually come to a room with an Exquisite Chest in the center. Walk into the room on the left, then activate the Pipe Diverter Valve to fill the Cascade Pool on the back wall. A Primal Ember will also appear to your left.

    Guide the Primal Ember to its next pedestal, then activate the next Pipe Diverter Valve. Continue to guide the ember through the now-opened door at the bottom of the staircase. We’ve made it to another cell puzzle room.

    To open the chest, rotate both cells in the room so that they’re both shooting beams into the Dual-Phase Neverlight Cell in the middle of the room.

    To open the barrier, rotate the cell on the right side of the image above so that it’s hitting the Dual-Phase Neverlight Cell in front of the barrier.

    Once the barrier is down, interact with the Pipe Diverter Valve on the other side. This will then unlock another valve for you to interact with. Activate both, then continue following the Primal Ember.

    In the next room, hit the switch to shut down the barrier, then guide the Primal Ember to its Primal Torch to make a Common Chest appear. Activate the Pipe Diverter Valve in this same room, then follow the markers back to where you started.
