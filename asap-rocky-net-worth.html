    Famous rapper, music producer, songwriter, and actor ASAP Rocky has generated a net worth of $10 million as of 2021.

    The multi-talented American Rapper was born in Harlem, New York City on October 3, 1988. He kicked off his career as a hip-hop collective A$AP Mob member; how he got his stage name, ASAP Rocky.

    Born as Rakim Athelaston Mayers, ASAP’s mother was a huge fan of the Rakim from the 80s hip-hop duo Eric B. & Rakim. Hence, the name.

    Meyers made rapping his profession in 2007 after joining the A$AP mob. Today he is one of the most renowned and most affluent among many rappers such as Jay-z, Travis Scott, etc.

    Inspired by his older brother, Ricky, ASAP Rocky started rapping at eight. Ricky was shot dead when ASAP was 13 years old, leading ASAP to take rapping more seriously.

    This followed a series of tragedy one being homeless with his mother and sister. This led him to sell cannabis and crack, but he continued to write rap songs.

    In 2004, Rocky served two weeks in prison for getting caught with dealing drugs. There he shared a cell with another future rapper, Cassanova.
