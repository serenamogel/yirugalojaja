    Katrina Kaif Biography & Career Summary

    Kaif also known by her birth name Katrina Turquotte was born in Victoria, Hong Kong on 16th July 1983 to English lawyer Suzanne Turquotte and British businessman Mohammed Kaif. According to her date of birth, she is 39 years of age as of 2022. She is British by nationality and Muslim by faith. She was then raised in London, England, United Kingdom.

    The Indian actress was not born alone. Her elder siblings include Stephanie Turcotte, Christine Raphael Duran Spencer, Natacha Turcotte, Melissa Turcotte Roberts, Sonia Turcotte, and Isabelle Kaif. Her younger siblings include Melissa, Sonia, and Isabel.

    At age 14, Katrina had already taken the first position in a Hawaiian beauty pageant. Whiles in London, she took a new career as a model and in 2013, started a new career as an actress in the Bollywood movie industry. The following are some of the movies she was featured.

    Who Is Katrina Kaif’s Husband?

    The Indian actress married Vicky Kaushal on 9th December 2021 at a resort in Sawai Madhopur, Rajasthan. Their marriage initially was kept private until their wedding pictures became public. In a statement, this is what Katrina had to say about keeping her wedding private.

    “I think more than trying to keep it private, we were restricted by, unfortunately, Covid-19. You know my family has been personally affected by Covid-19, and it was something that you had to take seriously.”

    She added, “I think this year it’s been a lot better, but it was something that we wanted to really be cautious of, but the wedding was beautiful. I think we are both very happy.”
