    Jar Of Hearts Lyrics

    Jar of Hearts is a popular song by American singer-songwriter Christina Perri, released in 2010 as the lead single from her debut album, Lovestrong. The song was a commercial success, reaching the top 5 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and receiving multi-platinum certifications in several countries.
