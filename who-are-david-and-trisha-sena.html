    Who Are David And Trisha Sena? Missing Girl Charlotte Sena's Parents

    Who are David and Trisha Sena? David and Trisha Sena are the parents of 9 years old Charlotte Sena who went missing last weekend while on a bike ride at Moreau Lake State Park in New York. New York Police announced on Monday evening that Charlotte had been found.

    29 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Who are David and Trisha Sena? – David and Trisha Sena are the parents of 9 years old Charlotte Sena who went missing last weekend while on a bike ride at Moreau Lake State Park in New York. New York Police announced on Monday evening that Charlotte had been found.

    New York State Police said in a statement,

    “Charlotte Sena has been located and in good health. A suspect is in custody. This is still an active investigation.”

    Who are David and Trisha Sena? 

    Charlotte Sena was born to David Sena (Father) and Trisha Sena (Mother) in Greenfield, a town of about 8,200 residents in Saratoga County, New York. Her father is a union pipe fitter whereas her mother is a food service inspector for Stewart’s Shops convenience stores. 

    Charlotte Sena is a 9-year-old 4th-grade girl who went missing from a state park in Moreau at the weekend on Saturday evening. When she went missing, she was wearing a tie-dye Pokémon shirt, blue pants, and black Crocs. Her mother said that her daughter is trusting and a good kid.

    Charlotte was reported missing after she didn’t return from a bike ride around 6:45 p.m. Her parents had been staying at the Park to wait for their daughter.

    Missing Girl Charlotte Sena’s Parents

    Charlotte’s mother  Trisha Sena said,

    “I just want my daughter back.”

    Their family friend Patrick Kane who has worked with Charlotte’s father David, said,

    “This family is hurting. You can’t imagine what the last 36 hours have been like for them. It’s devastating.”

    Charlotte’s aunt Jene said on Facebook,

    “Thank you so much to everyone who has reached out, as you can imagine we are all overwhelmed. Praying and sharing her photos is the best way anyone can help right now.”

    Jene was a producer at WNYT in the late 1990s and early 2000s.

    Missing Girl Charlotte Sena Was Found Safe

    Charlotte Sena was found safe on Monday. She is safe and in good health. Police captured a suspect who had allegedly abducted Charlotte.

    Police found the suspect Craig Nelson Ross after searching several residences on the basis of fingerprints for a ransom note.

    The suspect was arrested and taken into custody. Charlotte was found on Monday at about 6:30 p.m.

    Gov. Kathy Hochul said in a prepared statement,

    “We are overjoyed at the news that Charlotte Sena has been located safely this evening after an intense days-long search. Our hearts are with her family as they welcome her home.”

    Craig Nelson Ross Jr is a 46-year-old man who allegedly left a note at Charlotte’s parents’ home demanding ransom in connection with the 9-year-old girl’s disappearance.
