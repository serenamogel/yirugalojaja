    Walter Koenig Biography and Wiki

    Walter Koenig, born as Walter Marvin Koenig, is an American actor and screenwriter born and brought up in Chicago, Illinois, United States. Koenig is well known for his supporting role as Ensign Pavel Chekov in the American science-fiction series, Star Trek: The Original Series.

    Additionally, Walter has starred in several other films and television shows like Goodbye, Raggedy Ann (1971), The Questor Tapes (1974), Babylon 5 (1993), and Crusade (1999).

    Walter Koenig Education

    Koenig went to school in Manhattan, New York. Additionally, he attended Grinnell College in Grinnell, Iowa where he graduated with a pre-med major. Later on, he joined the University of California Los Angeles.

    He holds a Bachelor of Arts in psychology from UCLA. A professor encouraged Koenig to become an actor. Thus, he attended the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre in New York City. While in the Playhouse Theatre School, Walter studied alongside Dabney Coleman, Christopher Lloyd, and James Caan.

    Walter Koenig Startrek

    Koenig began playing Ensign Pavel Chekov, navigator on the USS Enterprise, in the original Star Trek television series in the second season and all of the films featuring the original cast, also including Star Trek Generations.

    One of only two actors to audition, he was cast as Chekov almost immediately primarily because he resembled British actor and singer Davy Jones of the Monkees. Show creator Gene Roddenberry hoped that Koenig would increase the show’s appeal to young people.

    (The studio’s publicity department, however, falsely ascribed the inclusion of Chekov to an article in Pravda that complained about the lack of Russians in Star Trek. As the 30-year-old’s hair was already receding, costume designers fashioned a Davy Jones-style “moptop” hairpiece for him. In later episodes, his hair grew out enough to accomplish the look with a comb-over.

    Walter Koenig Humanitarian

    In 2007, Koenig was asked by the human rights group U.S. Campaign for Burma to help in their grassroots campaign about the humanitarian crisis in Burma. As detailed on his official website, he visited refugee camps along the Burma-Thailand border from July 16 to 25, 2007.

    Walter Koenig Babylon 5

    Koenig plays the role of Alfred Bester in the film Babylon 5. Babylon 5 is an American space opera television series that premiered on January 26th, 1994, and ran for five seasons. According to the series, Alfred Bester is a senior officer of Psi-Corps.

    It follows a human military staff and alien diplomats stationed on a space station, Babylon 5. Many episodes focus on the effect of wider events on individual characters, with episodes containing themes such as personal change, loss, subjugation, corruption, defiance, and redemption.
