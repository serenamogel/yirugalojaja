    Bruno Tonioli Age, Height, Weight: Bruno Tonioli is an Italian choreographer, ballroom and Latin dancer, and Television personality born on November 25, 1955.

    He’s best known as a judge on ITV’s Britain’s Got Talent and Dancing with the Stars on ABC TV in the United States of America.

    Bruno Tonioli has also judged on the British television dance competition Strictly Come Dancing.

    He co-created and appeared on the BBC talent show DanceX and the American adaptation; Dance War: Bruno vs. Carrie Ann.

    In June 2021, it was announced that Tonioli would replace Anton Du Beke as a permanent judge on Strictly Come Dancing for series 19.

    It was then announced on 19 May 2022 that Tonioli would not be returning to Strictly and that Anton Du Beke would take over permanently.

    In October of the same year (2022), Tonioli competed in the second series of The Masked Dancer as Pearly King where he took third place.

    In December 2022, in the Strictly Come Dancing Christmas Special, Tonioli surprised viewers by singing “Don’t Leave Me This Way” by The Communards.

    In January 2023, Bruno Tonioli joined the judging panel on the British talent show Britain’s Got Talent.

    In April 2023, the panel’s latest addition, Tonioli made headlines after he pressed his golden buzzer halfway through an act’s performance on Saturday, April 15.
