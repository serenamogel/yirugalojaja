    Who is Skai Jackson?

    Skai Jackson is an American child actress.

    Skai Jackson: Professional Life, Career

    Skai Jackson made her debut in acting in the year 2005 at the age of five years old and appeared in the film titled “Liberty Kid” as a kid.

    In 2010, she made her T.V. debut and appeared in one episode of T.V series “Team Umizoomi”. Because of her minor role she didn’t get so much popularity.

    She also appeared in several low-rated films during the initial phase of her career including “Rescue Me”, “The Rebound”, “Royal Pains”, and “Boardwalk Empire.”

    In 2011, Skai Jackson starred in a successful T.V series “Bubble Guppies” as ‘Little Fish’ and starred in a total of 39 episodes until 2012.

    This was a solid base for her to start a professional career in the acting field.

    In 2015, Skai Jackson appeared in the T.V series “Bunk’d” as ‘Zuri Ross’ for which she was nominated for the ‘Shorty Award for Best Actress’ in 2016.

    Some of her latest films include “K.C. Undercover” (2015), “My Dad’s a Soccer Mom” (2015), and “Jessie’s Aloha-holidays with Parker and Joey” (2014), etc.
