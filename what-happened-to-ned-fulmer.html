    In late September 2022, the internet was abuzz with the news of Ned Fulmer, a founding member of the popular online comedy group, The Try Guys. Known for their entertaining videos where they try everything from new foods to fashion trends, The Try Guys built a loyal fanbase. However, the group’s dynamic changed dramatically when allegations surfaced about Ned Fulmer’s personal conduct. This article delves into the events that led to Fulmer’s departure from The Try Guys and the aftermath of the incident.

    The Rise of The Try Guys

    The Try Guys first gained popularity through their video series on BuzzFeed, where they worked before branching out to form their own independent production company in 2018. The group consisted of four members: Keith Habersberger, Zach Kornfeld, Eugene Lee Yang, and Ned Fulmer. They were known for their camaraderie and the family-friendly image they projected, with Fulmer often sharing anecdotes about his wife and children.

    Initial Rumors and Speculation

    In late September 2022, rumors began to circulate on social media platforms that Ned Fulmer had been involved in an extramarital affair. Fans noticed that Fulmer was conspicuously absent from recent Try Guys videos and social media posts, which fueled further speculation.

    Confirmation of the Allegations

    The Try Guys officially addressed the situation on September 27, 2022, confirming that Ned Fulmer was no longer working with the group. They released a statement explaining that Fulmer had engaged in a consensual workplace relationship, which they felt was a significant breach of their trust and the values of their company.

    Ned Fulmer’s Response

    Following the announcement from The Try Guys, Ned Fulmer released his own statement. He admitted to having a consensual relationship with a colleague and expressed regret for his actions. Fulmer also emphasized his commitment to focusing on his marriage and family.

    Public Reaction

    The news of Fulmer’s departure from The Try Guys elicited a wide range of reactions from fans and the public. Many expressed disappointment and shock, especially given Fulmer’s public persona as a devoted husband and father.

    Impact on The Try Guys Brand

    The Try Guys’ brand was built on their relatable personalities and group dynamic. The incident with Ned Fulmer raised questions about the future of the brand and how the remaining members would move forward without one of their founding members.

    Steps Taken by The Try Guys

    In the wake of the controversy, The Try Guys took several steps to address the situation and reassure their fanbase:

    • They removed Ned Fulmer from their social media accounts and website.
    • They edited Fulmer out of already filmed but unreleased content.
    • They held a meeting with their employees to discuss the situation and provide support.

    Media Coverage

    The incident received significant media attention, with many outlets reporting on the details of Fulmer’s departure and its implications for The Try Guys and digital content creators more broadly.

    Response from Other Try Guys Members

    The remaining members of The Try Guys, Keith Habersberger, Zach Kornfeld, and Eugene Lee Yang, released a video addressing the situation. They shared their feelings of betrayal and disappointment but also their determination to continue creating content for their fans.

    Impact on Ned Fulmer’s Personal Life

    While the full impact on Fulmer’s personal life remains private, his public statements suggest that he is working to repair his relationship with his wife and focus on his family.

    Discussion of Workplace Relationships

    The incident sparked broader discussions about workplace relationships, power dynamics, and the responsibilities of public figures to uphold certain standards of conduct.

    Future of The Try Guys

    The Try Guys have continued to produce content and engage with their audience. They have also been open about the process of moving forward and the challenges they face as a trio.

    Lessons Learned
